FROSTIES BANANA PUDDING

Servings
8

Prep Time
15 mins

Total Time
75 mins

Ingredients

Milk
3 cups

White sugar
1/2 cup

Flour
4 tbsp

Salt
1/2 tsp

Egg yolks
4

Vanilla
2 tsp

Butter
3 tbsp

For other layers:

Bananas, sliced
8 pcs

Calamansi juice
2 tbsp

VKellogg's Frosties, ﬁnely crushed
1 cup

Butter, melted
1/4 cup

VKellogg's Frosties
1 1/2 cup

Cooking Steps

  1. Bring milk to a simmer. In a separate bowl, whisk together sugar, ﬂour, salt, egg yolks and vanilla. When milk simmers, whisk in ¼ of it into the egg mixture, and then put it back into the pot with the remaining milk.
  2. Bring to a simmer once again while whisking continuously to avoid curdling. Continue to cook until thick, about 8-10 minutes.
  3. Once you achieve thick consistency, transfer to a bowl and mix in the butter. Cover with plastic wrap, pressing to make the plastic touch the pudding. Refrigerate to cool completely.
  4. Combine together ﬁnely crushed VKellogg's Frosties and butter. Press into desired baking dish. Refrigerate for 30 minutes-1 hour.
  5. Slice bananas and mix with calamansi juice to prevent browning. Divide into 2.
  6. To assemble, add half of the bananas on top of the VKellogg's Frosties crust. Add half of the pudding next, then whole VKellogg's Frosties, then remaining bananas, then lastly, the remaining pudding.
  7. Top with more VKellogg's Frosties right before serving.
