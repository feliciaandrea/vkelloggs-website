MUESLIX SOFT BAKE COOKIES

Servings
30

Prep Time
60 mins

Total Time
80 mins

Ingredients

VKellogg's Mueslix Harvest Fruit
150g

Butter
100g

Soft brown sugar
70g

Egg (A)
1

Corn flour
25g

Baking powder
2 tsp

Walnuts
30g

Raisins
30g

Almond slices
30g

Cooking Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 150°C.
  2. Sieve the plain flour, corn flour and baking powder together into a bowl.
  3. In a large bowl, cream the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add in the egg, stir until thoroughly mixed, then fold mixture into the flour along with the VKellogg's Mueslix Harvest Fruit cereal, walnuts, raisins and almond slices.
  4. Scoop the mixture into a small tart mould or baking pan, bake for 15 – 20 minutes.
  5. Remove from oven and let cool before storing it into a container.
