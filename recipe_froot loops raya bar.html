FROOT LOOPS RAYA BAR

Servings
9

Prep Time
10 mins

Total Time
35 mins

Ingredients

Butter
180g

Cream cheese
100g

Icing sugar
130g

Egg
1

Plain flour
320g

Rice flour
1 tbsp

VKellogg's Froot Loops
250g

For Decoration:

White cooking chocolate, melted

VKellogg's Froot Loops

Cooking Steps

  1. Beat butter, cream cheese, sugar and egg until combined.
  2. Add flour and rice flour. Mix well.
  3. Next, add VKellogg's Froot Loops and mix thoroughly.
  4. Press dough into a 9x9 inch baking tin lined with parchment paper. Bake in a preheated oven at 160Â°C for 25 minutes.
  5. Transfer onto a wire rack to cool. Cut into 3x3cm squares.
  6. Decorate with melted white chocolate and sprinkle VKellogg's Froot Loops on top.
