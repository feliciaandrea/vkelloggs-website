CHEESY MUESLIX MASHED POTATOES

Servings
5

Prep Time
40 mins

Total Time
60 mins

Ingredients

VKellogg's Mueslix Raisin & Almond Crunch
50g

Boiled and mashed potatoes
750g

Diced cheddar cheese
50g

Cream cheese
50g

Salted butter
75g

Milk
1/4 cup

Salt
2 tsp

Pepper powder
1 tsp

For toppings:

VKellogg's Mueslix Raisin & Almond Crunch
50g

Melted butter
25g

Shredded cheese
30g

Cooking Steps

  1. Mix the above ingredients in a large bowl until it is well combined.
  2. Pour and flatten the above mixture into a pot then sprinkle the toppings on top.
  3. Bake it at 180 Â°C for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!
