RAINBOW LOOP FLUFFY PANCAKE

Servings
6

Prep Time
25 mins

Total Time
45 mins

Ingredients

VKellogg's Froot Loops
2 cups

Plain flour
100g

Baking powder
2 tsp

Egg
1

Castor sugar
2 tbsp

Milk
180ml

Melted butter
2 tbsp

A pinch of salt

Cooking Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, egg, melted butter and milk in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan - when it's hot, spoon a ladle of pancake mixture into the pan.
  3. Sprinkle the VKellogg's Froot Loops on top and cook it until it is light brown.
  4. Take the pancake off the pan and place it onto a plate.
  5. Drizzle honey or butter on top and enjoy!
  6. Tip: The size of the pancake is flexible by using whatever utensils you have on hand. It can be big or small.
