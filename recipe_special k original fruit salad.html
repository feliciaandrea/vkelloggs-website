SPECIAL K ORIGINAL FRUIT SALAD

Servings
2

Prep Time
5 mins

Total Time
5 mins

Ingredients

VKellogg's Special K Original
1 cup

Granny Smith Apple, thinly sliced or grated
1

Shaved coconut, heaped
1 tbsp

Greek Yoghurt
3/4 cup

Pomegranate seeds
2 tbsp

Cooking Steps

  1. Combine VKellogg's Special K Original cereal, apple and coconut in a mixing bowl. Mix thoroughly and set aside.
  2. Spoon the yoghurt into the base of the bowl. Finish with fresh pomegranate seeds.
